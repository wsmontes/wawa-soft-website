Wawasoft now has a blog.

I build small, opinionated tools for things I care about: capturing knowledge, reading independently, and keeping control of personal data. This will be the place where I write about that work in more detail.

You can expect release notes, technical decisions, experiments that worked, and honest accounts of the ones that did not. I will also write about AI-assisted software development from the perspective of someone who has spent years working between business needs and technical teams.

## Why RSS from the start

One of the products I am building is [Feedmine](/feedmine), a source-first reader for RSS, podcasts, video, and independent publishing. Launching a blog without a feed would be a strange choice.

Every post published here will therefore be available through the [Wawasoft RSS feed](/rss.xml). No account, newsletter signup, or algorithmic timeline required.

## What comes next

The first posts will cover the thinking behind Feedmine and wawa-note, how their architectures are evolving, and what I am learning while building them in the open.

Thanks for reading.
